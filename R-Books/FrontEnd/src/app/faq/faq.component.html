Frequently Asked Questions

Online Order FAQs

1. What is an online order?
An Online Order is a textbook or merchandise order placed through our UStore website.
2. Why should I place an online order?
Ordering online provides the customer with the luxury of selecting clothing, other merchandise and textbooks from the comfort of your own home or whereever you are when you visit our site. During the semester, ordering your textbooks online through gives you the most updated materials required at the most reasonable price. We even provide you with the prices of our competitors so you can decide for yourself. Not to mention online ordering guarantees you the materials you need before the books even hit the shelf.
3. How do I place an online order?
To place an online order go to and click on the tab on the left that says “LOG IN.” If you have not yet created a profile, select “REGISTER HERE” and fill out the necessary information or continue shopping as a Guest. If you do have a profile, please log in and begin shopping!
4. What is an Order Number?
The ORDER NUMBER is the confirmation number that is given when placing an order. It is also contained in the subject of the confirmation email received when the order has been successfully placed. The ORDER NUMBER is a six digit number that you will need to pick up or gain information about your order. If you do not receive an email with a confirmation / order number then the order has not been successfully placed. (NOTE: BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR SPAM WHEN LOOKING FOR YOUR CONFIRMATION EMAIL).
5. Is the cost the same as buying a book in the University Store?
Yes the price stays the same whether you order online or come in and buy an item.
6. If I place an order online can I have it shipped to me? How much is it to ship an order? Once my order is processed how long will it take to ship?
Yes, we ship via USPS or UPS. Shipping is currently a flat-rate of $7.99 for most orders. Delivery times vary based on carrier and location.
7. Can I place an order online and come pick it up? Where do I pick it up?
Yes, we do have a PICK UP AT STORE option. The PICK UP AT STORE option is located in STEP 3 (DELIVERY METHODS) of the checkout process. Please visit the UStore to claim your order once you receive confirmation that it's ready.
8. How long does an order take to process?
We strive to place orders in a timely fashion. During busy times, like the beginning of a semester or around holidays, additional processing time is to be expected.
9. When picking up an online order, what information do I need?
A PHOTO ID and your ORDER NUMBER are required to pick up your online order.
10. I placed an order online and have not received an update to the status of my order. What do I do?
